EatWell

 

The majority of people do not know what they should be eating. They listen to doctors, nutritionists, their peers, and whatever they find on the internet. But do people really know what they should be eating and how frequently? People don't usually know how to properly shop around the store. There are a lot of unhealthy advertisements. A lot of people do care about healthier options, but they are never given the healthy options that resonate most with their body and blood types.
