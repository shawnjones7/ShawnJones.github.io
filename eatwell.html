EatWell
Personalized to your type

 

EatWell is a service that creates meal and lifestyle recommendations based on a home service blood test that you provide.
These recommendations are geared around information that is collected based on your blood type and the information the individual inputs upon initiating program. The service recommends recipes and diet changes to improve health and congnitive ability based on the output from EatWell's AI. The service is geared for individuals 18-35 years of age, who are interested in nutrition, but have little information about what to do. I was tasked to design a recommended recipe page for one of EatWell's customers basd on the data outputted from the AI below and the recipe details. The goal of this page was to show the customer the recipe and why it's recommended, while making it easy to understand. I decided to not just design this page, but to design the homepage and how the customer get's to this recipe page. I feel that it is important to be able to map out the entire situation. This would make sure I wasn't missing anything along the way and make sure I keep a seemless experience while navigating throughout the website.
