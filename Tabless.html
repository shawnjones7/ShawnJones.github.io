Tabless App

 

Have you ever been to a bar and sat there for waiting for your order? What about your check? Have you accidently left your credit card at the bar? If your answer was yes to any of those questions, then you are in the majority of every other bar goer. Tabless is an app that will eliminate all of your worries, so instead of waiting around, you can be having fun and enjoying your time with your friends at the bar. Through Tabless you can preview the restaurant, check out their menu, check-in while at the bar, and order your drinks. Your drinks will always be served to you with little hassle and at a much faster rate than ordering through a server. When you are done, you can check out on your app and keep bar hopping. What if I forget to check out before leaving, well, we have you covered. Once you leave the geolocation of the bar, then your tab will be automatically checked out with the tip amount that was inputted during your profile setup. This app not only helps out customers but is exceptional for bars and restaurants too. Tabless speeds up the serving rate, increases sales and revenue, and cuts cost on servers.
