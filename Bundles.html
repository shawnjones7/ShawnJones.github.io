Bundles Mobile App

 

Bundles is a roadmap and curriculum building platform. It works as a CMS that tags learning activities so that they dynamically load courses based on your role and learning outcome. I was given the opportunity to give a fresh look and redesign of Bundles responsive mobile application. My goal was to make an intuitive experience for users to simply choose a role and explore courses within that role. We focused on the ability to track and display users progress throughout their learning journey.
